Shaken & Stirred.

Shakken & Stirred is a sophisticated, vibrant but playful cocktail tasting event. The event is planned to happen at Junkyard Market in Norwich in summer 2025.

With cocktail making workshops, a DJ lineup and store to make the cocktails at home, this festival-like experience invites people to trial new flavours while having a good time with friends.
