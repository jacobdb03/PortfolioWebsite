Concept Project

Conductor.

The design of a brand for a rail transportation service with a theme of classical music. Informed from the insight that “98% of passengers listen to music while on the train, with 40% of respondents saying that not listening to music would negatively impact their mood and mental wellbeing," as well as the many studies that have concluded that classical music has a calming effect on people (data from the Rail Delivery Group).

Brief set by Conran Design Group
