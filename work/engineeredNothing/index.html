D&AD Newblood

Swiss-Engineered Nothing.

A minimalist, human-centred campaign answering the On brief for D&AD Newblood 2025 that asked to tell On's story through movement to a new generation using the tagline: "Feel Nothing. To Feel Everything."

By juxtaposing their apparel with Gen Z’s boundless energy, On provides the 'Nothing' — lightweight, distraction-free technology designed to be forgotten.

Meanwhile, the wearer brings the 'Everything' — the passion, ambition, and spirit that brings On's story to life.

On silences life’s distractions, allowing Gen Z to focus on what matters most — the freedom of movement.
