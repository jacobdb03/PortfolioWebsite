Live Brief

The Good Eggs.

This project is the effort of 9 developers, designers and business-people to create an online system that can connect farmers with and audience, cutting out the middlemen supermarkets to provide a better wage to farmers and fresher produce to customers. This won the people's choice award at sync the city 2024 — A competition to develop a startup business idea from scratch in 54 hours.
